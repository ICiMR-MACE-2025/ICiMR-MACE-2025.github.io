# About ICiMR 2025

The 3rd International Conference on Intelligent Manufacturing and Robotics (ICiMR 2025) marks the third edition of this conference series, which was incepted in 2023 by the School of Robotics and the School of Intelligent Manufacturing, Xiâ€™an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU). Sunway University is proud to host the 2025 edition in collaboration with Universiti Teknologi Malaysia.

ICiMR 2025 aims to provide a vibrant platform for academics, researchers, industry professionals, and relevant stakeholders to share, discuss, and deliberate on their latest research findings and technological advancements in the fields of intelligent manufacturing and robotics.

The conference welcomes submissions in, but not limited to, the following non-exhaustive topics aligned with its overarching themes of intelligent manufacturing and robotics:


## Intelligent Manufacturing

- Smart factories and Industry 4.0 applications
- Digital twin technology for manufacturing
- Cyber-physical production systems
- Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT)
- Predictive maintenance and condition monitoring
- Sustainable and green manufacturing technologies
- Advanced process control and optimisation
- Human-robot collaboration in manufacturing environments


## Robotics

- Autonomous mobile robots and applications
- Human-robot interaction and collaboration
- Robot learning and adaptive control
- Swarm robotics and multi-agent systems
- Robotic vision and perception
- Soft robotics and compliant mechanism
- Robotics for precision agriculture
- Robotics for healthcare and rehabilitation


## Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics

- Machine learning applications in manufacturing
- Deep learning for quality inspection and defect detection
- Data-driven decision-making in production systems
- AI for robotic path planning and control
- Edge and cloud computing for smart manufacturing


## Advanced Materials and Mechatronic Systems

- Smart and functional materials for robotics
- Additive manufacturing and 3D printing
- Sensors and actuators in mechatronic systems
- Embedded systems for intelligent manufacturing
- Micro- and nano-manufacturing


## Emerging Themes

- Ethics, safety, and governance in robotics and intelligent manufacturing
- Education and training for intelligent manufacturing and robotics
- Technology adoption and readiness in SMEs
- Cross-disciplinary applications in intelligent manufacturing and robotics
